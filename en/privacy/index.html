Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 19, 2025

tl;dr

We do not sell your data. We are here to recommend tech, not trade user profiles.

We use minimal cookies. Only standard analytics to see how many people visit the site.

No accounts needed. You don’t need to log in to use One Final Choice.

1. Information We Collect

One Final Choice (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates onefinalchoice.com. We prioritize minimalism in data collection.

Log Data: Like most websites, our servers automatically record information when you visit. This may include your IP address, browser type, device type, and the pages you visit.

Analytics: We may use privacy-focused tools (such as Google Analytics or Vercel Analytics) to understand site traffic. These tools may use cookies (small text files stored on your device) to collect anonymous usage data.

Voluntary Communication: If you email us or contact us via social media (e.g., X.com), we collect the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Information

We use the data collected strictly to:

Maintain and improve the performance of the website.

Analyze trends (e.g., which recommendations are most popular).

Detect and prevent technical issues or fraud.

3. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (e.g., manufacturer pages, retailers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

4. Your Rights (GDPR & KVKK)

Depending on your location, you have rights regarding your data, including the right to request access to or deletion of your personal data. Since we do not store personal user accounts, our ability to identify you is limited to standard web logs.
