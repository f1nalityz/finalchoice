Mouse

A single mouse choice for daily computer use.
Translations:

Recommendation#

  • Model: Logitech G Pro X Superlight

Why this exists#

This mouse prioritizes precision, comfort, and low friction. It is light enough to reduce fatigue and accurate enough to disappear during use.

It works equally well for work and casual gaming without adjustment.

This is for you if#

  • You want a mouse that feels effortless
  • You value precision and consistency
  • You don’t want to think about peripherals

This is not for you if#

  • You need programmable macro grids
  • You prefer very heavy mice
  • You want an unconventional shape
