Principles

The principles behind making fewer, better choices.
Translations:
  • Tr

Most people don’t need better options. They need fewer.

This site exists to remove unnecessary choice and the regret that follows it.

What I optimize for#

  • Stability over peak performance
  • Longevity over novelty
  • Predictable behavior over flexibility
  • Fewer tools over perfect tools

What I ignore#

  • Benchmarks without context
  • Influencer recommendations
  • Marginal gains that increase complexity
  • Products that require constant attention

What I refuse to do#

  • Provide comparison lists
  • Offer multiple “good” options
  • Recommend things I wouldn’t trust long-term

How to use this site#

  • If a choice fits you, take it and move on
  • If it doesn’t, don’t compromise
  • Hire me only if you need a final answer

This is not a review site. It is a stopping point.
